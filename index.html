Citizens Office of Communications Safety

If you have nothing to hide, you have nothing to fear.

The Citizens Office of Communications Safety protects all citizens from the threat of information deviancy by monitoring digital communications and recording acts of abnormal knowledge transfer.

Citizens Office of Comunications Saftey

If you witness any unlawful or suspicious information transmission please inform the Citizens Office of Communications Safety or contact the nearest law enforcement officer.

All communications monitoring is permuted under the 2014 statute 'Communications Reporting Assistance Program' included within section 4c of the Teriyaki act.
